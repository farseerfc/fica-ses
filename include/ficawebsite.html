Welcome to Fica!

Fica is a survey about source code clones in softwares.

We need your help!

The survey will take around 2 hours approximately. You can suspend in the middle and come back at any time with your convenience. Although to keep a consistency among collected data, we strongly recommanded you to finish the survey with in one day.

How to participate?

To participate the survey, please register as a new user in the following form.

If you have registered, please login from the right top corner.

Register a new user(required)

Agree the Privacy Agreement

About Register

Fica is a system for research purpose only. Fica tried very hard to study the habit of each user and provide individual result according to the study.

In that sense, We need to collect some information of every surveyee in this research, so that we can determined the parameters that affected the study result of Fica, and improve our result.

Privacy Agreements

  1. Privacy Informations collected by Fica
    • Username (Required)

      Nickname is sufficient.

    • Email Address (Required)

      To inform you about the important notice of Fica only.

    • Password (Required)

      To login into Fica system.

    • Gender (Optional)

    • Age (Optional)

    • Major in college/university (Optional)

    • Programming Experience (Optional)

    • Programming Language (Optional)

    • C Language Experience (Optional)

    • Knowledge on Code Clone (Optional)

    And your IP address are only recorded as access log.

  2. Fica will NEVER publish your Email address or password in any form.

    We will only use your Email address internally in some circumstances such as when follow up survey are needed or analysis result of your data is ready. And your password is kept only by you.

  3. Fica WILL publish your selections on clonesets. But Fica will NEVER publish your selection data associated with your privacy data other than username.

    Because of that, please select a nickname as username that is hard to trace your privacy.

  4. We will NEVER sell your any data to third parties.

    We will only publish your selection data, in altered format so that it is impossible to trace your privacy, to academic.

  5. You are free to change your optional privacy data.

    After login, you can click the "Change Information" button under your login-ed username.

  6. You have the rights to change your required privacy data.

    As for username and Email address, you can changed them by sending a Email to me. This requires some modification directly into database so you can not change it on this website.
